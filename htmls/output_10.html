Instructions:

Please watch each video and rate the videos based on Four evaluation metrics,
1. Prompt following: How well does the video follow the prompt
2. Motion Quality: Whether the motion in the video is smooth and natural
3. Camera Move: Whether the video present the camera move in a natural way
4. Overall: Overall quality of the video
Please rate each video on a scale of 1 to 5, where 1 is the lowest and 5 is the highest
